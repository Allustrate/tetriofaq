Is TETR.IO Desktop installation getting stuck? Here's a workaround!

You can use a *good* archiving program, such as 7zip(<https://www.7-zip.org/download.html>), to manually extract the game's contents for a much more manual installation for now. Here's a quick video guide(really, just open the `.exe` as a compressed folder and drag stuff out!): https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/673305614318960671/904141134752784434/use_7zip.mp4
